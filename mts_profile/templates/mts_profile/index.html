About

  • INTRODUCTION

    MyTech Systems Solutions is a  Zambian owned ICT Company which was established in August 2019 with the objective to provide product and services in the area of networking. MTS Solutions places greater emphasis in understanding the customer need , budget and resources. This enables us to design a cost effective approach towards developing a solution.

  • VISION

    To be the leading providers of world class Local Area Networking and Security Surveillance.

  • MISSION

    To help our clients achieve network connectivity at all times so that they will remain highly competitive in their respective area of business.

RANGE OF SERVICES

IT CONSULTING

Offering strategic advice and guidance to clients on IT infrastructure , systems , and technology solutions.

MANAGED IT SERVICES

Providing ongoing monitoring , maintenance , and support for clients' IT systems. 

NETWORK AND DESIGN IMPLEMENTATION

Designing and setting up secure and efficient computer networks for business.

CCTV
